Computer Science 2

You should learn some things about queues when you write this program. In particular, you should learn that

queues are FIFO data structures

adding to a queue is called "enqueue"

enqueues always happen at the end of the queue

removing an item from a queue is called "dequeue"

dequeues always happen at the fron of the queue

like stacks, you can't add to a full queue

like stacks, you can't remove something from an empty queue
